Heuristic Evaluation

An Heuristicevaluation of the website Utiligroup: www.utiligroup.com for Hannah's University Project.
Heuristic Evaluation Report

Date: 2nd April 2020
Name: Hannah Reardon

System: Utiligroup Website: https://www.utiligroup.com/
About: The website Utiligroup is a market leader in the UK energy market. They provide automated software, expert services and data insight to aid energy companies to enter the market.

The evaluation uses 3 of Nielsen (1994b) Heuristic Evaluators with A set of 3 of the heuristic evaluators are selected to evaluate the website: Visibility of system status, Match between system and the real world, Aesthetic and minimalist design. From this two personas are created of people who would access the website with a desired goal.
