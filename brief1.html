Heuristic Evaluation

An Heuristicevaluation of the website Utiligroup: www.utiligroup.com for Hannah's University Project.
Heuristic Evaluation Report

Date: 2nd April 2020
Name: Hannah Reardon

System: Utiligroup Website: https://www.utiligroup.com/
About: The website Utiligroup is a market leader in the UK energy market. They provide automated software, expert services and data insight to aid energy companies to enter the market.

The evaluation uses 7 of Nielsen (1994b) Heuristic Evaluators: Visibility of system status, Match between system and the real world, Aesthetic and minimalist design, error prevention, user control and freedom, consistency and standards and recognition rather than recall. From this two personas are created of people who would access the website with a desired goal. Below is the Capian screen shots of the evaluation and the personas used.
